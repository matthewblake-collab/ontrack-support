Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's exactly what we collect, why, and how it's protected.

Last updated: April 2026

OnTrack Focus is built by a solo developer. We collect only what's necessary to run the app. We never sell your data. We never share it with advertisers.

What we collect

  • Email address and display name (used for your account)
  • Apple ID sign-in token (if you use Sign in with Apple)
  • Habit logs, supplement logs, and daily check-in responses you enter
  • Session attendance and group membership data
  • Apple Health data (sleep, steps, heart rate) — only if you grant permission
  • Device push token — used only to send you notifications you opt into
  • Profile photo — only if you choose to upload one

How we use it

  • To provide the core app features — habit tracking, groups, sessions, supplements
  • To generate personalised AI wellness insights from your check-in history
  • To send push notifications for sessions, reminders, and friend activity
  • To display your progress, streaks, and personal bests
  • To enable group accountability features like leaderboards and shared sessions

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

Apple Health data

If you grant HealthKit permission, OnTrack Focus reads sleep, step count, and heart rate data to pre-fill your daily check-in. This data is processed on-device and stored only in your OnTrack account on our servers. We never share HealthKit data with third parties and never use it for advertising.

You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → OnTrack Focus.

AI wellness insights

When you request an AI insight, your recent check-in data (sleep, energy, wellbeing scores) is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a personalised response. This data is processed transiently and not stored by Anthropic beyond their standard API data handling. You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com.

Where your data is stored

Your data is stored on Supabase (supabase.com), a secure cloud database platform. Data is stored in US-based data centres with encryption at rest and in transit. We use Supabase's row-level security to ensure users can only access their own data.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Supabase — for database hosting (data processor only)
  • Anthropic — transiently for AI insight generation (no persistent storage)
  • Apple — for push notification delivery via APNs
  • When required by law

Your rights

  • Access your data — all your data is visible within the app
  • Delete your account — go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. All your data is permanently removed.
  • Export or correct your data — contact us at the email below
  • Withdraw HealthKit consent — via iPhone Settings at any time

Children's privacy

OnTrack Focus is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll update the date above and notify users via the app where appropriate. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
